## Submission Policy

- Submit through Canvas/Gradescope
- Include both report and code
- Submission deadlines are posted on the schedule page

## Collaboration and Integrity

- You may discuss ideas with classmates, but all submitted work must be your own.
- You must cite any external resources (papers, libraries, tools, AI assistants) that materially informed your solution.
- Directly copying solutions or requesting complete solutions from others/tools is prohibited.

## Late Policy (placeholder)

- Each student receives a limited number of late days.
- Late-day usage limits per assignment and project milestone will be announced.
- After late days are exhausted, a grade penalty applies per additional day.
